ABOUT US

We are a company built for small business owners. 

Our goal is to eduate small business owners on the importance of technology for the survival of their company. We want to bring our experience using technology at large companies to the many uses cases within a small business lifecycle. 

We are starting locally within Massachussetts, helping small businesses leverage technology as they begin to reopen during this coronavirus quarauntine period. The world has been forced onto digital platforms, and small businesses must adapt quickly.
